Short Bio

I am a geospatial data analyst, urban planner/technologist, and architect-engineer interested in data analysis & visualizations, Big Data, urban analytics, urbanism, sustainable strategies, and policy making.

I have been working on projects and ideas in the light of the human factor and the intersection of urban & regional planning/analysis and sustainable strategies with data-driven approaches. Formating sustainable and circular strategies for the future of the cities and the built environment are essential elements in my analytical process.
